How to Play

Objective

Navigate through 20 challenging levels by solving puzzles and managing your resources. Complete each level before time runs out while maintaining your health to progress to the next challenge.

Gameplay

  1. Select a level from the level selection screen
  2. Use the main game canvas to interact with game elements
  3. You'll receive a protective shield for 5 seconds every 10 seconds
  4. Your controls will invert every 10 seconds
  5. Answer terminal questions correctly to restore normal controls
  6. Solve terminal puzzles to progress through levels
  7. Monitor your timer and health bar carefully
